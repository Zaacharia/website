About Us Page


Overview


Our team has started Sinelangan as a school project that capitalizes on making cinema reservations through an online website due to the health uncertainty of different individuals from the pandemic disease. Having Sinelangan lessens the physical interaction and long queue that may threaten health concerns from our customers. Sinelangan pursues excellence and equity as the representation of the team’s core values. Sinelangan is led by its founders, Ajay Matthew Lorenzana, John Carlo Reyes, Francis Santos, and Jireh Teope, who formed the functionality and design of the website with the aim of serving our customers to the best of our abilities.
